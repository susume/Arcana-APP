Which deck are you reading with?

Arcana can interpret traditional tarot cards or playing cards used for cartomancy. Choose the deck in your hands so card meanings and suits match your reading.

Traditional Tarot

Use this for a 78-card tarot deck with 22 Major Arcana and 56 Minor Arcana across Wands, Cups, Swords, and Pentacles.

Playing Cards

Use this for a standard 52-card deck. Arcana reads Hearts, Diamonds, Clubs, and Spades through classic cartomancy meanings.

Playing Cards + Joker

Use this for a 53-card deck. The Joker adds themes of possibility, risk, surprise, and trust in the unknown.
