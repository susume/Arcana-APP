What is Arcana?

Arcana is an AI tarot reading app for physical cards

Arcana is built for people who already own a tarot deck or want to practice with real cards. Instead of simulating a random draw, it helps you choose a spread, lay out your own cards, upload a photo, and receive an interpretation shaped by the positions in your spread.

How does an AI tarot reading work?

You focus on a question, draw cards from your own deck, photograph the spread, and let Arcana identify each card before creating a reflective reading.

What can a tarot reading tell you?

A reading can highlight emotions, patterns, choices, and possible next steps. It is best used as a mirror for reflection, not as a fixed prediction.

Which tarot spread should I choose?

Use one card for a quick daily focus, three cards for past-present-future context, and deeper spreads when a situation has several moving parts.
